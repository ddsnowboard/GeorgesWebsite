About Us

The attorneys of Seltzer & Seltzer, L.C. have practiced law in the St. Louis metropolitan area since the late 1970’s. The firm has always provided clients with efficient and effective representation in wide range of civil practice areas and also developed an expertise in construction and collection law. The lawyers of Seltzer & Seltzer serve a broad range of clients in various business and personal endeavors while maintaining a special concentration in mechanic’s lien and payment bond claims. Seltzer & Seltzer provides comprehensive representation with complete and focused attention to the needs of the client.
